Intresting Facts About Penguin

    Penguin wings have the same general bone structure as flighted birds, but the bones are shorter and stouter to allow them to serve as fins.

    The emperor penguin has the largest body mass of all penguin

    The emperor penguin has the largest body mass of all penguins,which further reduces relative surface area and heat loss.

    They also are able to control blood flow to their extremities, reducing the amount of blood that gets cold, but still keeping the extremities from freezing. In the extreme cold of the Antarctic winter, the females are at sea fishing for food, leaving the males to brave the weather by themselves. They often huddle together to keep warm and rotate positions to make sure that each penguin gets a turn in the center of the heat pack.
